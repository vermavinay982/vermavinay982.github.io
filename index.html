Vinay K. Verma

I am currently working as a Research Assistant at IIIT Delhi, pursuing a Master's by Research under the guidance of Prof. Dr. AV Subramanyam. My research focuses on 3D segmentation and the development of edge-optimized, high-accuracy models for satellite and medical image segmentation. As part of this work, I presented at IIITD’s flagship research showcase, RIISE 2024, and continue to explore challenges in network design, optimization, and deployment on constrained hardware.

Prior to this, I worked as a Computer Vision Engineer in the industry, contributing to products at Wobot Intelligence, PixelBin by Fynd (a Reliance Jio-backed startup), and StatsPerform. My experience spans CCTV-based surveillance systems, large-scale media transformation tools, and sports analytics solutions involving re-identification, tracking, and sunlight-invariance in football footage. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication from GGSIPU, where I also worked on robotics and IoT projects—an experience that laid the foundation for my passion in computer vision and AI.
